Focus RS

History

The Focus Rs had a short but exciting run in the United States, lasting all of 3 years from 2016-2019. The first true "Hot Hatch" from Ford and some say it was a tribute to the 1996 Ford Escort Cosworth a true legend and highly sought after rally car in Europe, they get all the cool toys. The 2016 Focus RS sported an 2.3l EcoBoost engine which is capable of putting down 350 HP and 350 TQ to go along with it's AWD drivetrain. The RS will easliy do 13.3 in the 1/4 mile and 0-60 in under 4.5 seconds making it a car enthusists dream for the track.
